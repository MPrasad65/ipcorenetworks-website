About IPCORE Networks

IPCORE Networks is a technology-driven networking and IT infrastructure solutions provider, delivering reliable, secure and scalable solutions for businesses, apartments, commercial complexes, ISPs and enterprise networks.

We specialize in designing, deploying and supporting LAN, WAN and complete network infrastructure solutions. Our services include internal cabling, CCTV surveillance solutions, security device installations and complete IT infrastructure setup based on customer requirements.

We provide professional network configuration and support services for Local Cable Operators (LCOs), Class B & Class C networks, ISPs and enterprise customers. Our expertise includes network design, routing, switching, firewall configuration, troubleshooting and ISP consultancy services.

IPCORE Networks also provides network equipment sales and services, offering new and refurbished devices from leading vendors including Cisco, Huawei, Juniper, MikroTik, D-Link, TP-Link and other networking brands.

Our product portfolio includes routers, switches, firewalls, managed and unmanaged switches, wireless networking devices, optical networking solutions, various OLT platforms and complete network infrastructure components.

We also provide customized computer hardware solutions including desktop PCs and IT equipment sales based on customer requirements.

With practical industry experience and vendor expertise, IPCORE Networks supports ISP networks and enterprise customers through consulting, freelancing, network design, implementation and end-to-end infrastructure solutions.
